ABOUT COUNTRYCLIFF

The CountryCliff Landscape Architects was founded by Justin CountryCliff in 1987. Our experienced landscaping consultants offer a complete landscape design service. We specialise in the creation of exclusive, distinctive, environmentally-sustainable landscape architectural and urban design solutions. Our company is based in the western suburbs of Nowhere and our services extend to the Nowhere Metropolitan Area and the surrounding country areas of Nowhere.

As landscape architects and urban designers we aim to achieve functionality, amenity, environmental sustainability and the provision of social equity through our work - they are built to be shared.

There will be an application of a unique blend of science, art and environmental-sensitivity to the projects we create. We recognise the cultural and social influences that affect human behaviour and response to our environments. We focus alot on connecting our clients to   their gardens.

Services we offer: Site Appraisal, Client Briefing, Environmental Assessment, Visual Assessment and Impact, Landscape Concept Design, Gardening maintenance
